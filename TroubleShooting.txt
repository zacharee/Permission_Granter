	Device will not show up-
1. make sure adb is enabled and your phone is plugged into your pc
2. try a new usb cable or a new port on your pc

	I cant find usb debugging-
1. Go into your settings on your phone and go to about device, 
once there find your build number and click it untill it says 
developer settings have been enabled. Once that is done go 
back to the main settings page and developer settings should 
be shown and you can go into them and enable usb debugging
